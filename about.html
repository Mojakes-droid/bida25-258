About Us

Where we come from and what we want to achieve

HANDLY's was founded in 2016 in the backyard of an individual named Mojakes. It started small, but there was a clear vision for the business. With strong goals guiding its growth, it has developed into one of the leading automotive customization companies in Botswana. HANDLY's is driven by a passion for excellence in every car it handles, striving to deliver exceptional quality and personalized service to every customer.
